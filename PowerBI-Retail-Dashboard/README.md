# Power BI Retail Sales Dashboard

## Overview

Interactive Power BI dashboard built to monitor retail sales, profitability, customer behavior, and product performance.

## Dashboard Pages

### Executive Dashboard

* Revenue KPI
* Profit KPI
* Orders KPI
* Customers KPI
* Revenue by State
* Profit by State
* Revenue Trend

### Customer Insights

* Revenue by Age Group
* Revenue by Education
* Revenue by Marital Status
* Revenue by Pet Ownership

### Product Performance

* Revenue by Product Category
* Revenue by Sub Category
* Top Customers
* Product Profitability

## Business Insights

* California is the largest revenue-generating state.
* Household Appliances drive most revenue.
* Adult customers contribute the highest revenue.
* TV products dominate sales performance.
